<p><p>The cancer pain therapeutics market is categorized into three main types: opioids, non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), and others. Opioids, such as morphine and oxycodone, are powerful analgesics used for managing moderate to severe pain. NSAIDs, like ibuprofen and naproxen, provide relief for mild to moderate pain and help reduce inflammation. The "others" category encompasses adjuvant therapies, including anticonvulsants and antidepressants, which are utilized to alleviate cancer pain by targeting nerve-related discomfort or enhancing the effects of primary pain medications.</p></p>
