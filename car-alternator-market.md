Integral Alternators combine the generator and rectifier into a single unit, optimizing space and efficiency. Pumping Alternators, designed for specific applications, can enhance fluid circulation in hybrid or electric vehicles. Each type caters to unique automotive needs, driving innovation and performance in vehicle electrical systems.</p></p>
