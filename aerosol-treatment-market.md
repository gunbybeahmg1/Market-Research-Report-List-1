<p><p>The aerosol treatment market comprises various devices designed for delivering medication directly to the respiratory system. Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) administer a specific dose of aerosolized drug with a pressurized canister. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) release medication in powder form that patients inhale. Nebulizers convert liquid medicine into a mist for easier inhalation, particularly aiding those with severe respiratory issues. Other categories may include soft mist inhalers and nebulizing systems, highlighting the diversity in aerosol delivery methods for respiratory therapies.</p></p>
