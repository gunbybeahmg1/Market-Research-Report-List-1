<p><p>Function-as-a-Service (FaaS) is a cloud computing execution model that allows developers to run individual pieces of code in response to events without having to manage server infrastructure. This serverless architecture enables businesses to focus on writing code while the cloud provider handles scaling, availability, and resource management. FaaS has become increasingly popular due to its cost-effectiveness, ease of use, and ability to support microservices architecture, leading to faster development cycles and improved agility.</p><p>The Function-as-a-Service Market is expected to experience significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for serverless computing, the rise of IoT applications, and the need for rapid application development. Organizations are increasingly adopting FaaS for its scalability and efficiency, allowing them to pay only for the execution time of their functions. Additionally, the integration of FaaS with machine learning and data analytics is enhancing its appeal across various industries. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period, as more businesses seek to leverage its benefits for streamlined workflows and improved operational performance. Emerging trends include increased vendor offerings and a focus on multi-cloud environments, further fueling market expansion.</p></p>
