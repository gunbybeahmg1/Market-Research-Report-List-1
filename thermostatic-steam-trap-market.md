<p><strong>The Thermostatic Steam Trap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bi-metal Strip Type</li><li>Balanced Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermostatic steam trap market features two main types: Bi-metal Strip Type and Balanced Pressure Type. The Bi-metal Strip Type operates using a bi-metallic element that bends in response to temperature changes, efficiently discharging condensate and preventing steam loss. In contrast, the Balanced Pressure Type utilizes a balanced mechanism of pressure to manage condensate and steam separation, ensuring optimal performance in varying conditions. Both types offer reliable solutions for effective steam management in industrial applications, enhancing energy efficiency.</p></p>
