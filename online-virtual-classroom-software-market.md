<p><p>The Online Virtual Classroom Software market is divided into two primary types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a user's hardware, offering greater control and customization, but requiring significant maintenance and upfront costs. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the platform from any device with internet connectivity. This model often includes flexible subscription plans and automatic updates, making it more scalable and convenient for educational institutions.</p></p>
