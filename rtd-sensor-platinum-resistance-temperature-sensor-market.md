<p><p>RTD sensors, or Resistance Temperature Detectors, utilize platinum to measure temperature with precision. The two primary market types are wire-wound and thin film RTD sensors. Wire-wound RTDs consist of thin platinum wires wrapped around an insulating core, offering high stability and accuracy, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, thin film RTDs feature a platinum sensing element deposited on a ceramic substrate, providing faster response times and compact designs. Both types serve diverse industrial needs, ensuring reliable thermal measurement.</p></p>
