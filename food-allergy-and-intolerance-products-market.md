<p><p>The Food Allergy and Intolerance Products Market is segmented into two main types: Immunoglobulin E (IgE) Mediated Food Allergy and Non-IgE Mediated Food Allergy. IgE Mediated Allergies trigger an immediate immune response, leading to symptoms like hives or anaphylaxis, commonly associated with foods like peanuts and shellfish. In contrast, Non-IgE Mediated Allergies, such as food protein-induced enterocolitis syndrome, involve delayed responses, causing gastrointestinal issues. Each type requires tailored products for management, influencing market demand and innovation.</p></p>
