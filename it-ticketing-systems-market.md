<p><p>IT ticketing systems are classified into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise ticketing systems require organizations to host and manage the software on their local servers, offering full control but necessitating significant IT resources and infrastructure. In contrast, cloud-based ticketing systems are hosted on external servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and ease of access without the need for extensive IT management. Cloud solutions often feature automatic updates and lower upfront costs, appealing to various organizational needs.</p></p>
