<p><p>The fingerprint sensor market comprises three main types: capacitive, optical, and thermal sensors. Capacitive sensors capture detailed fingerprint patterns using electrical charges, providing high accuracy and durability. Optical sensors use light to capture images of fingerprints, offering broader application versatility but may be less secure. Thermal sensors detect heat differences between the ridges and valleys of fingerprints, adding a layer of security and being less susceptible to fake fingerprints. Each type serves unique needs across various industries, enhancing security and user experience.</p></p>
