<p><p>The fiber optic patch cables market is primarily divided into two types: singlemode and multimode fiber optic patch cables. Singlemode cables utilize a single light path and are designed for long-distance communication, offering high bandwidth and low signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunication networks. In contrast, multimode cables have multiple light paths and are suitable for shorter distances, providing higher bandwidth at reduced costs, making them favored in local area networks and data centers. Both types serve critical roles in high-speed data transmission.</p></p>
