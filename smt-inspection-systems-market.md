<p><p>SMT inspection systems encompass various technologies to ensure quality in electronics manufacturing. Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI) detects defects in solder joints and hidden components by visualizing internal structures. Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) uses cameras to inspect surface mount devices for defects like missing components or misalignments, focusing on the visual integrity of assemblies. Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) measures the volume and placement of solder paste before components are placed, ensuring optimal conditions for reliable connections in assembly processes.</p></p>
