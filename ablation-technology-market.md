<p><p>Ablation technology encompasses various modalities for tissue removal or destruction in medical procedures. Electrical ablation uses heat generated by electrical currents to target and eliminate tissue. Ultrasound ablation employs high-frequency sound waves to create thermal effects for tissue destruction. Radiation ablation utilizes ionizing radiation to target and destroy abnormal cells, often used in cancer treatments. Radiofrequency ablation applies radio waves to generate heat, effectively treating conditions like arrhythmias or tumors by precisely targeting and ablating tissue.</p></p>
