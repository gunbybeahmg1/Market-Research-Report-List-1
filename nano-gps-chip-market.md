<p><ul><li>Low Power</li><li>Sensitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nano GPS chip market comprises two main types: low power and sensitive GPS. Low power GPS chips are designed for battery-operated devices, prioritizing energy efficiency while maintaining adequate positioning accuracy. These are ideal for wearables and IoT applications. In contrast, sensitive GPS chips focus on high precision and performance in challenging environments, such as urban canyons or dense forests. They are essential for applications requiring precise location tracking, like autonomous vehicles and advanced navigation systems.</p></p>
