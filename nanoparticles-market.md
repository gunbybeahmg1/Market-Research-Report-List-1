<p><p>The nanoparticles market encompasses various types that serve distinct applications in drug delivery and therapeutics. Liposomes are spherical vesicles that encapsulate drugs for targeted delivery. Polymer-conjugated drugs combine therapeutic agents with polymers for improved stability and efficacy. Polymeric nanoparticles offer controlled release mechanisms. Dendrimers are branched macromolecules that provide high drug loading capacity and customizable surfaces. Inorganic nanoparticles, like gold or silver, excel in imaging and diagnostics. Other categories include silica and carbon-based nanoparticles, contributing to diverse medical applications.</p></p>
