<p><p>The drug labels market includes various types such as pressure-sensitive labels, glue-applied labels, and others. Pressure-sensitive labels use an adhesive that bonds upon application, allowing for easy labeling without additional heat or moisture. Glue-applied labels require the application of adhesive during the labeling process, providing a strong bond but often requiring more time and equipment. Other types encompass assorted label technologies, such as heat transfer or direct printing methods. Each type serves unique needs in pharmaceutical packaging and compliance.</p></p>
