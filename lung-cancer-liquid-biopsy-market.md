<p><strong>The Lung Cancer Liquid Biopsy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exosomes and RNA</li><li>CTCs and ctDNA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lung cancer liquid biopsy market comprises three main types: exosomes and RNA, circulating tumor cells (CTCs), and circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA). Exosomes and RNA involve the analysis of extracellular vesicles to identify molecular markers, providing insights into tumor biology. CTCs capture intact cancer cells from the bloodstream for characterization and treatment monitoring. ctDNA focuses on analyzing fragments of tumor-derived DNA in circulation, enabling non-invasive detection of genetic mutations and real-time monitoring of disease progression or response to therapy.</p></p>
