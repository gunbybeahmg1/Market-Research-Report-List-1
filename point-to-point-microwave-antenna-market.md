<p><p>The point-to-point microwave antenna market comprises several types, primarily including parabolic antennas and flat panel antennas. Parabolic antennas feature a curved reflector that focuses signals, providing high gain and directivity, ideal for long-distance communication. Flat panel antennas, on the other hand, use a flat design for a sleek appearance and are simpler to install, offering a wider beamwidth. Other types may include phased array or dish antennas, catering to specific needs and applications in telecommunications and data transmission.</p></p>
