<p><p>The hydraulic torque pump market is primarily segmented into air-driven and electric-driven types. Air-driven torque pumps utilize compressed air to generate hydraulic power, making them suitable for hazardous environments where electric sparks could pose risks. They offer portability and ease of use. In contrast, electric-driven torque pumps rely on electric motors to provide hydraulic force, delivering consistent performance and efficiency for larger operations. Both types serve diverse industrial applications, with selection depending on specific operational needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
