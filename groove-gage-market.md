<p><ul><li>Digital Display</li><li>Pointer Display</li><li>Caliper Reading</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Groove Gage market encompasses three primary types: digital display, pointer display, and caliper reading. Digital display gauges offer precise measurements with easy readability and often include advanced features like data storage. Pointer display gauges provide traditional analog readings, relying on a mechanical needle for measurement, appealing for simplicity and durability. Caliper reading gauges combine elements of both, using a scale for direct measurement, catering to users who prefer hands-on, tactile approaches in their precision tasks. Each type serves specific user preferences and application needs.</p></p>
