<p><p>The Plastic Optical Fiber (POF) market encompasses two primary types: PMMA (Polymethylmethacrylate) and perfluorinated polymers. PMMA fibers are widely used due to their high transparency, ease of processing, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for short-distance communication and lighting applications. In contrast, perfluorinated polymers exhibit superior performance in demanding environments, offering excellent chemical resistance and lower attenuation for high-speed data transmission. Both types cater to diverse applications, including telecommunications, automotive, and medical sectors, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
