<p><p>The isolated power panel market comprises two main types: Simplex and Three Phase systems. Simplex isolated power panels operate on a single-phase supply, typically used in smaller applications where simplicity and cost-effectiveness are crucial. In contrast, Three Phase isolated power panels utilize a three-phase supply, providing higher power capacity and efficiency, making them suitable for larger facilities or industrial settings. Both types enhance safety and reliability by preventing ground faults and ensuring continuous operation in sensitive environments.</p></p>
