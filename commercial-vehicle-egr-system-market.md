<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) System market includes two primary types: electric and pneumatic EGR valves. Electric EGR valves use electric actuators for precise control of exhaust gas flow, enhancing efficiency and facilitating integration with advanced engine management systems. In contrast, pneumatic EGR valves rely on air pressure to modulate exhaust gas recirculation, often providing a simpler, more robust solution for traditional engine designs. Both types are essential for reducing emissions and optimizing engine performance in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
