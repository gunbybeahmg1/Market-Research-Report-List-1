<p><p>The cement wall putty market is divided primarily into two types: interior wall putty and exterior wall putty. Interior wall putty is formulated for indoor applications, providing a smooth finish, enhancing wall aesthetics, and improving paint adherence. It effectively hides imperfections and offers moisture resistance. In contrast, exterior wall putty is designed for outdoor surfaces, offering superior durability against weather conditions, UV rays, and moisture. It protects and beautifies external walls, ensuring long-lasting performance and reducing maintenance requirements.</p></p>
