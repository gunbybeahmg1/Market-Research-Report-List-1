<p><p>The fractionated fatty acid market consists of three main types: Very Long-Chain Fatty Acids (VLFA), Medium-Chain Fatty Acids (MCFA), and Long-Chain Fatty Acids (LCFA). VLFA, often used in specialized applications, include fatty acids with more than 22 carbon atoms. MCFA, typically derived from coconut oil, are known for quick absorption and energy production, found in dietary supplements. LCFA, with 12 to 22 carbon atoms, are commonly used in food, cosmetics, and industrial applications for their stability and emulsifying properties.</p></p>
