<p><p>The space propulsion systems market is categorized into three main types. Solid fuel chemical propulsion uses solid propellants, offering simplicity and reliability, ideal for launch vehicles. Liquid fuel chemical propulsion involves liquid propellants, providing high thrust control and efficiency, suitable for advanced spacecraft maneuvers. Cold-gas chemical propulsion operates using inert gas, delivering low thrust ideal for attitude control and small satellite applications. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing performance, complexity, and cost in space missions.</p></p>
