<p><p>The medical laser systems market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Diode Laser Systems** are compact and efficient, suitable for various medical applications including dermatology and surgery. </p><p>2. **Solid State Laser Systems** utilize solid gain mediums and offer high power output, often used in ophthalmology and aesthetic procedures. </p><p>3. **Dye Laser Systems** employ organic dyes as the medium, providing versatile wavelengths for treatments like skin rejuvenation. </p><p>4. **Gas Laser Systems**, using gases like carbon dioxide, are effective for cutting and vaporizing tissue in surgery. Each type plays a critical role in advancing medical treatments.</p></p>
