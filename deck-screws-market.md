<p><p>The deck screws market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. Flathead screws feature a countersunk design for a flush finish, making them ideal for smooth surfaces. Trim head screws provide a low-profile appearance, often used in visible areas. Truss head screws offer a wider bearing surface, suitable for soft materials. Hex head screws allow for higher torque applications, while round head screws provide aesthetic appeal and are easier to drive into wood. Each type caters to distinct construction and aesthetic needs.</p></p>
