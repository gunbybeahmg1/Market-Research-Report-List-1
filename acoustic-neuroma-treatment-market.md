<p><p>The acoustic neuroma treatment market is categorized into two main types: unilateral and bilateral acoustic neuromas. Unilateral acoustic neuromas, which affect only one ear, typically involve surgical options, radiation therapy, or monitoring, depending on the tumor's size and symptoms. In contrast, bilateral acoustic neuromas, often associated with neurofibromatosis type II, require more complex treatment strategies, including multiple surgeries or advanced therapies due to the presence of tumors in both ears. These distinctions influence market demand and treatment approaches.</p></p>
