<p><strong>The Mercury Poisoning Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chelating Agents</li><li>Combination Therapy with Chelating Agents</li><li>Plasma Exchange Hemodialysis Plasmapheresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mercury poisoning treatment market includes various approaches to mitigate toxicity. Chelating agents, such as dimercaprol and DMSA, bind to mercury, facilitating its excretion from the body. Combination therapy with chelating agents enhances detoxification efficiency, often used for severe cases. Plasma exchange involves removing blood plasma containing mercury for replacement, while hemodialysis and plasmapheresis focus on filtering toxins directly from the bloodstream. Each method targets mercury elimination, tailored to the severity of poisoning and patient health.</p></p>
