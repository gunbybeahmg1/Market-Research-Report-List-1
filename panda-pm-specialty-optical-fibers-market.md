<p><p>PANDA PM specialty optical fibers are categorized into two main types: multimode and single-mode PM fibers. Multimode PM fibers are designed to support multiple light modes, making them suitable for short-distance applications with high bandwidth requirements, while providing polarization maintenance. Conversely, single-mode PM fibers allow only one light mode to propagate, enabling long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss and preserving polarization. Both types are essential for advanced communication systems requiring precise control of light polarization.</p></p>
