<p><strong>The Bellows Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bellows Stop Valve</li><li>Bellows Gate Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bellows valve market includes two primary types: bellows stop valves and bellows gate valves. Bellows stop valves feature a flexible metal bellows that prevents leakage, making them ideal for high-pressure and temperature applications, ensuring safe and reliable operation. Bellows gate valves, on the other hand, are designed for on-off control with minimal pressure drop, utilizing a gate mechanism. Both types are essential in various industries, including oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment, due to their durability and leak-proof capabilities.</p></p>
