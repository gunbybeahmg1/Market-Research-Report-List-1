<p><p>Steel fibers in the underground market are crucial for enhancing the performance of concrete, especially in mining and tunneling applications. Cold-drawn steel wire fibers are produced through cold drawing processes, resulting in high tensile strength and durability, making them ideal for reinforcement. Cold-drawn shaved steel fibers, on the other hand, are created by shaving cold-drawn wire, offering improved surface characteristics and bond strength with concrete. Both types effectively reduce cracking, improve toughness, and enhance overall structural integrity in underground constructions.</p></p>
