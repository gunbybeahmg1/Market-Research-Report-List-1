<p><p>Digital-to-Analog Converters (DACs) convert digital signals into analog signals, essential in various applications like audio, video, and instrumentation. Market types include R-2R DACs, which use a resistor ladder for precise voltage output; string DACs, leveraging a series of resistors for better linearity; current source and sink DACs, which manage current flow for applications involving current feedback; and other specialized DACs catering to specific requirements like data acquisition or signal modulation. Each type serves unique performance and application needs.</p></p>
