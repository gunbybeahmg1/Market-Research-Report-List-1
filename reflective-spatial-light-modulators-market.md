<p><ul><li>Dielectric Mirror Type</li><li>No-Dielectric Mirror Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective Spatial Light Modulators (SLMs) are categorized into two main types based on their optical structure: Dielectric Mirror Type and No-Dielectric Mirror Type. The Dielectric Mirror Type utilizes multi-layer dielectric coatings to reflect light efficiently, enhancing modulation depth and image quality, making it suitable for high-end applications like holography and advanced displays. In contrast, the No-Dielectric Mirror Type relies on reflective surfaces without additional coatings, offering cost advantages for simpler applications but with potentially lower performance in light control and resolution.</p></p>
