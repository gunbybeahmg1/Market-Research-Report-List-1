<p><p>The bioresorbable polymer-based implants market comprises several key materials. Polyglycolic Acid (PGA) offers rapid degradation, ideal for short-term applications. Polydioxanone is known for its excellent elasticity and slower resorption rate, while Polycaprolactone (PCL) is favored for its prolonged degradation time, making it suitable for long-term implants. Other materials in this market include blends and copolymers designed for specific biomedical applications.</p></p>
