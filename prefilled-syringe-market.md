<p><ul><li>Glass Prefilled Syringes</li><li>Polymer Prefilled Syringes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prefilled syringe market comprises two primary types: glass prefilled syringes and polymer prefilled syringes. Glass prefilled syringes offer superior chemical compatibility and stability, making them ideal for biologics and sensitive medications. However, they are heavier and more fragile. In contrast, polymer prefilled syringes are lighter and less prone to breakage, providing greater patient convenience and safety. They are gaining popularity due to their flexibility in design and reduced risk of contamination, catering to a diverse range of therapeutic applications.</p></p>
