<p><p>The commercial drones market is segmented into three main types: fixed-wing drones, rotary blade drones, and hybrid drones. Fixed-wing drones are designed for long endurance and efficient coverage over large areas, making them ideal for mapping and agricultural applications. Rotary blade drones, or quadcopters, offer greater maneuverability and are commonly used for aerial photography, inspections, and delivery services. Hybrid drones combine the strengths of both designs, allowing vertical takeoff and landing capabilities alongside long-distance flight efficiency, catering to diverse commercial needs.</p></p>
