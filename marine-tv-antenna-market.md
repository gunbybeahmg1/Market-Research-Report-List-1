<p><ul><li>Omnidirectional</li><li>Orientation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine TV antenna market is primarily divided into two types: omnidirectional and orientation antennas. Omnidirectional antennas are designed to receive signals from all directions, making them ideal for boats that move frequently or lack a fixed position. Conversely, orientation antennas require directional alignment towards a signal source, offering enhanced reception and clarity but needing manual adjustment. Each type caters to specific user needs based on mobility, signal stability, and installation preferences, ensuring diverse options for marine entertainment solutions.</p></p>
