<p><ul><li>Co-Operative Technology</li><li>Non-Cooperative Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UAV Sense-and-Avoid Systems are classified into two main market types: Cooperative and Non-Cooperative technology. Cooperative technology involves systems that rely on information exchange between UAVs and other aircraft, typically using transponders or ADS-B, enhancing situational awareness. In contrast, Non-Cooperative technology operates independently, utilizing sensors like radar, LIDAR, or cameras to detect and avoid obstacles without external assistance. Both technologies aim to improve UAV safety during operations in shared airspace, catering to different operational needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
