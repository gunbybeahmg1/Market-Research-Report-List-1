<p><p>The 3D medical imaging equipment market encompasses various modalities critical for diagnostics. X-Ray is widely utilized for visualizing bone structures and detecting abnormalities. Ultrasound employs sound waves for real-time imaging of soft tissues, primarily during pregnancy. MRI uses strong magnetic fields to produce detailed images of organs and tissues, especially the brain and joints. CT Scan combines X-ray technology for cross-sectional images, enhancing comprehensive diagnostics. Hybrid imaging integrates different techniques, improving accuracy and treatment planning in clinical practices.</p></p>
