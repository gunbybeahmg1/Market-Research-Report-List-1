<p><strong>The Hydraulic Wiper Seal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic wiper seal market is segmented into single-acting and double-acting types. Single-acting wiper seals are designed to operate with fluid pressure only on one side, effectively preventing contaminants from entering the hydraulic cylinder during extension. In contrast, double-acting wiper seals function on both sides, accommodating fluid pressure in both directions, which provides enhanced protection against external debris regardless of the cylinder's movement. These seals are crucial for ensuring the longevity and efficiency of hydraulic systems.</p></p>
