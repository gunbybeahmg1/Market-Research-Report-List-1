<p><strong>The Anti-vibration Table Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Anti-vibration Table</li><li>Active Anti-vibration Table</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-vibration table market comprises two primary types: passive and active anti-vibration tables. Passive anti-vibration tables use mechanical systems, such as springs and dampers, to absorb vibrations and stabilize equipment. They are typically more affordable and require less maintenance. In contrast, active anti-vibration tables employ advanced technology, including sensors and actuators, to actively counteract vibrations in real-time, providing superior stabilization for sensitive instruments. Both types are essential in laboratories and industrial applications, ensuring precise measurements and enhanced performance.</p></p>
