<p><p>The genetic engineering tool enzyme market encompasses several key types essential for DNA manipulation. DNA restriction endonucleases cut DNA at specific sequences, facilitating cloning and gene editing. Ligases join DNA fragments, crucial for constructing recombinant DNA. Polymerases synthesize DNA strands during replication and amplification processes. Nucleases degrade nucleic acids, enabling genome editing and repair. Modification enzymes, including methyltransferases and phosphatases, chemically modify DNA, enhancing its stability and function. Together, these enzymes drive advancements in biotechnology and genetic research.</p></p>
