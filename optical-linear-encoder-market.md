<p><strong>The Optical Linear Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axle Type</li><li>Shaft Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical linear encoder market is primarily divided into two types: axle type and shaft type. Axle type encoders are designed for applications where the movement is along a linear path, typically in automation and robotics, providing accurate position feedback. Shaft type encoders, on the other hand, are used in rotating applications, translating rotational movement into linear measurements. Both types rely on optical sensors to detect incremental changes, ensuring high precision and reliability in various industrial and engineering applications.</p></p>
