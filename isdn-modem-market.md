<p><strong>The ISDN Modem Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Basic Rate Interface</li><li>Primary Rate Interface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ISDN modem market consists of two primary types: Basic Rate Interface (BRI) and Primary Rate Interface (PRI). BRI is typically used for smaller businesses and residential connections, providing two 64 Kbps bearer channels and one 16 Kbps signaling channel, enabling simultaneous voice and data transmission. In contrast, PRI is designed for larger organizations, offering 23 bearer channels and one signaling channel, allowing for greater bandwidth and supporting multiple simultaneous calls, making it ideal for high-demand environments.</p></p>
