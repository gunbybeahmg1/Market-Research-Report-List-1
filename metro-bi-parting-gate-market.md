<p><strong>The Metro Bi-Parting Gate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Movement</li><li>Double Movement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metro bi-parting gates are designed for efficient pedestrian access in transit systems. The market is categorized into two types: single movement and double movement. </p><p>In single movement, one gate leaf operates at a time, allowing for controlled passage, which is ideal for low to moderate foot traffic. Alternatively, double movement gates feature two leaves that can open simultaneously, accommodating higher volumes of people and providing quicker access, making them suitable for busy metro stations. Both types enhance overall flow and security.</p></p>
