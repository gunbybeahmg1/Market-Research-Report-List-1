<p><strong>The Electric Actuator for Aircraft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Motion</li><li>Rotary Motion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric actuators for aircraft are essential components used for controlling various systems. The two primary types are linear motion and rotary motion actuators. Linear motion actuators convert electrical energy into straight-line movement, commonly used in control surfaces like flaps and landing gear. In contrast, rotary motion actuators create rotational movement, essential for operating systems such as rudders, ailerons, and throttle controls. Both types enhance aircraft performance, improve efficiency, and increase reliability in diverse flight operations.</p></p>
