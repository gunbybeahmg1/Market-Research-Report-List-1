<p><p>The Agricultural Vortex Pump market includes three main types: Closed Vortex Pumps, which minimize cavitation and offer high efficiency for irrigation and drainage; Open Vortex Pumps, characterized by a simple design that allows for handling larger solids while maintaining a smooth flow; and Centrifugal Vortex Pumps, which combine the principles of centrifugal force with vortex action for enhanced performance in moving fluids.
