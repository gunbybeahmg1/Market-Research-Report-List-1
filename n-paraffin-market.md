<p><ul><li>Below C10 N-Paraffin</li><li>C10-C13 N-Paraffin</li><li>C14 and Above N-Paraffin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The N-Paraffin market is categorized based on carbon chain length. Below C10 N-Paraffins are used mainly in the production of lubricants and solvents, characterized by their low viscosity and volatility. C10-C13 N-Paraffins serve applications in personal care products and detergents, providing emollient and surfactant properties. C14 and above N-Paraffins are utilized in industrial applications, such as coatings and high-performance lubricants, offering enhanced stability and higher melting points. Each type caters to specific needs across various industries.</p></p>
