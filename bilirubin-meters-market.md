<p><strong>The Bilirubin Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bench-top</li><li>Transcutaneous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bilirubin meters market is divided primarily into two types: bench-top and transcutaneous devices. Bench-top bilirubin meters are laboratory-based systems that provide accurate and precise measurements of bilirubin levels in blood samples, making them suitable for clinical settings. In contrast, transcutaneous bilirubin meters are non-invasive devices that estimate bilirubin levels through the skin, offering a quick and painless alternative for screening, particularly in newborns. Both types serve essential roles in monitoring jaundice and other liver-related conditions.</p></p>
