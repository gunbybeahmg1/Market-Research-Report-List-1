<p><p>Bone cerclage wiring involves the use of materials to stabilize fractured bones. Key market types include UHMWPE (Ultra-High Molecular Weight Polyethylene) fibers, which offer flexibility and high tensile strength, making them ideal for reducing tissue irritation. Metal wires, typically stainless steel, provide strong and durable fixation but may cause higher tissue response. Other polymers encompass various synthetic materials that combine strength and biocompatibility, catering to diverse surgical needs and preferences in orthopedic applications.</p></p>
