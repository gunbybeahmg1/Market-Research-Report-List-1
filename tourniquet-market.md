<p><p>The tourniquet market is primarily divided into two main types: latex and TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are traditionally used for their elasticity and ease of application, but they may pose allergy risks for some individuals. Conversely, TPE tourniquets are gaining popularity due to their hypoallergenic properties, durability, and environmental benefits, offering a safer alternative. Both types are essential in medical settings for controlling bleeding and facilitating various procedures, catering to diverse user needs and preferences.</p></p>
