<p><p>Suspension lift kits enhance vehicle ground clearance and off-road performance. **Spring suspension lift kits** utilize upgraded springs for added height and improved load-bearing capacity. **Coil suspension lift kits** replace factory coils with higher coils, optimizing ride quality and articulation. **Shackle suspension lift kits** involve extending the leaf spring's shackle to raise the vehicle, providing a simpler and cost-effective solution. **Others** may include air suspension or adjustable kits, catering to diverse preferences and vehicle requirements across the market.</p></p>
