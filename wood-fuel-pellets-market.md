<p><p>The wood fuel pellets market is primarily divided into two types: white pellets and black pellets. White pellets are manufactured from clean, processed wood sources, offering higher energy content and lower emissions, making them ideal for residential heating and industrial applications. In contrast, black pellets are produced from biomass feedstocks that retain some moisture and are more suitable for co-firing with coal in power plants due to their higher ash content. Both types cater to renewable energy demands and reduce reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
