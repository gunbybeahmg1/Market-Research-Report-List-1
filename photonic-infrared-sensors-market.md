<p><strong>The Photonic Infrared Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid Wave Infrared</li><li>Long Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photonic infrared sensors market is segmented into three main types based on wavelength ranges. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) sensors, operating between 0.9 to 2.5 micrometers, are ideal for applications like industrial inspection and food sorting. Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR) sensors, covering 3 to 5 micrometers, are commonly used in thermal imaging and surveillance. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) sensors, ranging from 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in thermal detection and are widely utilized in security, environmental monitoring, and automotive applications.</p></p>
