<p><p>The electrostatic fieldmeter market is divided into two main types: non-contacting and contacting. Non-contacting fieldmeters measure electrostatic fields from a distance, allowing for safe and convenient evaluation of static electricity levels without direct surface contact. In contrast, contacting fieldmeters require direct interaction with the surface to measure static charge accurately. Both types serve critical roles in industries like electronics, manufacturing, and cleanrooms, providing essential data for managing static electricity and ensuring product safety and quality.</p></p>
