<p><ul><li>"Compression Nebulizer"</li><li>"Ultrasonic Nebulizer"</li><li>"Mesh Nebulizer"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nebulizer inhalation therapy device market features three main types: compression nebulizers, ultrasonic nebulizers, and mesh nebulizers. Compression nebulizers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into an aerosol for inhalation, making them widely used and cost-effective. Ultrasonic nebulizers utilize ultrasonic vibrations to produce mist, offering quieter operation and faster delivery times. Mesh nebulizers employ a mesh mechanism to create aerosols, providing portability and efficient drug delivery with minimal waste, appealing to both patients and healthcare providers.</p></p>
