<p><p>The Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) market comprises three main types: Fire-rated MDF, Moisture Resistant MDF, and General MDF. Fire-rated MDF is engineered to resist flames, making it suitable for fire-sensitive environments such as commercial buildings. Moisture Resistant MDF is treated to endure high humidity levels, ideal for kitchens and bathrooms. General MDF serves a broad range of applications, offering versatility and affordability for furniture, cabinetry, and interior design projects. Each type caters to specific needs within construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
