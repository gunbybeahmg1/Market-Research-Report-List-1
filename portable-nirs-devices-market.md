<p><strong>The Portable NIRS Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FT-NIR Devices</li><li>UV-Vis-NIR Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable NIRS (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy) devices market encompasses FT-NIR (Fourier Transform Near Infrared) and UV-Vis-NIR (Ultraviolet-Visible-Near Infrared) devices. FT-NIR devices utilize Fourier Transform technology for rapid analysis, offering high sensitivity and customizable spectral resolution, making them ideal for a wide range of applications, including pharmaceutical and food industries. UV-Vis-NIR devices combine ultraviolet, visible, and near-infrared light analysis, catering to diverse measurement needs, from material identification to concentration determination, promoting accessibility and efficiency in field operations.</p></p>
