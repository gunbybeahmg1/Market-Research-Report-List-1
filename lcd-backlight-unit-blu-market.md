<p><p>The LCD Backlight Unit (BLU) market comprises two main types: Edge Type and Direct Type. Edge Type backlighting involves LEDs placed along the edges of the screen, providing a slim design and efficient light distribution. This type is commonly used in thinner displays. In contrast, Direct Type backlighting features LEDs positioned directly behind the LCD panel, offering uniform brightness and better color accuracy, making it suitable for high-quality screens and applications requiring precise visual performance. Both types cater to different display needs and preferences.</p></p>
